Our Story

Built by Students, For Students

Founded in 2019, Heads Up Mentors started with a simple belief: every student deserves access to mentoring, regardless of background or circumstance. As a student-led organization, we know what it's like to need guidance — and we're here to provide it.

We provide completely free mentoring focused on empowering a new generation through teaching, connection, and community. We've grown to reach students across multiple countries and continue to expand our impact.

We believe that ALL students deserve the same educational opportunities to succeed.
